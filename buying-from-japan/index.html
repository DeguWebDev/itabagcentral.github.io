Buying From Japan

There are many places that you can find merch at! However not all will ship internationally. Here are a few pointers for buying from Japan!

Recommended Proxies

To buy from stores that do not ship internationally, you will need to use a proxy (or middle-man).

  • From Japan
  • Zenmarket
  • Buyee
  • Tenso
  • Otsukai (community-based proxy)
  • Treasure Japan (for Mercari)

Note that all of these will charge a fee for their services. Be sure to research which will work best for you!

Japan-Only Sites

  • Yahoo Japan Auctions
  • Mercari Japan
  • Suruga-Ya
  • Otamart
  • Booth (fanart and doujinshi)
  • Animate
  • Movic

There are more than this, and possibly there's a store just for your series! Search around and see what you can find, and you can always request your proxy buy from it.

No Proxy Needed!

If you don’t want to go the route of using a proxy, here are a few sites that ship internationally!

  • AmiAmi
  • Mandarake
  • Rakuten
  • Hobby Link Japan
  • Hobby Search
  • CD Japan
  • Animate International
  • Goods Republic
  • Jadedou
